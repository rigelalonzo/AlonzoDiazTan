WALK IT TIL' YOU MAKE IT!

Paul Walker Application is an ambitious lifestyle doing nothing alert app.It serves as an alarm clock that
helps students monitor how much of their time they are doing nothing or sitting. And it detects their movements
if they are moving or just sitting around. And if a given time threshold is achieved, then they are required to move or
walk around to stop the alarm and will only be turned off if the user will walk around for a few minutes.
